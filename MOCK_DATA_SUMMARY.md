## Overview

## Data Statistics

### KPI Categories
- **5 Main Categories**: Safety, Quality, Production, Cost, People
- Each category has its own color scheme and icon

### Metrics Per Category
**Total: 15 Metrics across all categories**

#### Safety (3 metrics)
1. **No of Accidents** - Count of workplace accidents
2. **Near Misses Reported** - Count of near-miss incidents
3. **Safety Training Completion %** - Percentage of completed safety training

#### Quality (3 metrics)
1. **Right First Time @ Quality** - First-pass yield percentage
2. **Customer Complaints** - Count of customer complaints
3. **Defect Rate (PPM)** - Parts per million defect rate

#### Production (3 metrics)
1. **Production Attainment** - Production target achievement
2. **OEE %** - Overall Equipment Effectiveness percentage
3. **Downtime Hours** - Equipment downtime hours

#### Cost (3 metrics)
1. **Energy Consumed** - Energy consumption in units
2. **Overtime Hours** - Total overtime hours worked
3. **Scrap Cost ($)** - Cost of scrapped materials

#### People (3 metrics)
1. **Absenteeism** - Employee absence count
2. **Employee Turnover %** - Turnover rate percentage
3. **Training Hours per Employee** - Average training hours

## Data Patterns and Trends

### Safety Metrics
- **Seasonal Pattern**: Higher accident rates in winter months (weeks 1-12, 48-52)
- **Improvement Trend**: Year-over-year reduction in accidents (2022 → 2023 → 2024)
- **Near Misses**: Increased reporting indicating better safety culture
- **Training Completion**: Gradual improvement from 80% (2022) to 90%+ (2024)

### Quality Metrics
- **Upward Trend**: Right First Time percentage improving from 78% (2022) to 87-93% (2024)
- **Customer Complaints**: Decreasing trend from 4+ (2022) to 2-3 (2024)
- **Defect Rate**: Significant improvement from 150+ PPM (2022) to 50-150 PPM (2024)

### Production Metrics
- **Cyclical Pattern**: End-of-quarter peaks (weeks 13, 26, 39, 52)
- **OEE Improvement**: Steady growth from 68% (2022) to 75-90% (2024)
- **Downtime Reduction**: Decreased from 15+ hours (2022) to 5-15 hours (2024)

### Cost Metrics
- **Seasonal Variation**: Higher energy consumption in winter and summer
- **Overtime Reduction**: Declining from 120+ hours (2022) to 50-150 hours (2024)
- **Scrap Cost**: Reduced from $3000+ (2022) to $1000-4000 (2024)

### People Metrics
- **Seasonal Absenteeism**: Spikes during flu season (winter) and summer vacations
- **Turnover Improvement**: Decreased from 3.5% (2022) to 2.5% (2024)
- **Training Investment**: Increased from 1.2 hours (2022) to 2+ hours (2024)
