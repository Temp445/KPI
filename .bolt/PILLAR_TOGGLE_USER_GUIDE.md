# Pillar Number Toggle - User Guide

## What's New?

The small colored boxes inside each KPI card's large letter icon now display sequential numbers that change based on your selected time period.

### Step 1: Locate the Time Period Toggle
Look at the top of the dashboard for three toggle buttons:
- **Daily**
- **Weekly** (default)
- **Monthly**

### Step 2: Select Your Preferred View

#### Daily View
Click the **Daily** button to see:
- Numbers 1-28, 1-29, 1-30, or 1-31
- Count automatically adjusts based on selected month

## Common Questions

### Q: Why do I only see 12 boxes?
**A**: The pillar display shows the first 12 numbers for visual clarity. This represents a sample of the selected time period.

### Q: Why does February show different numbers?
**A**: February has 28 days in regular years and 29 days in leap years (like 2024).

### Q: How do I know if a year has 52 or 53 weeks?
**A**: Simply select the year and click "Weekly" - the system automatically calculates the correct number of weeks.

### Q: Do the charts below change?
**A**: No, the charts continue to display your actual KPI data. Only the pillar numbers change based on your toggle selection.

### Q: Can I switch between modes?
**A**: Yes! Click any of the three buttons (Daily, Weekly, Monthly) at any time to change the display.
